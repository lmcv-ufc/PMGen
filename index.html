Plane Mesh Generator (PMGen) is an academic mesh generation software for planar regions capable to generate structured and unstructured finite element meshes. Both traditional elements (T3, T6, Q4, and Q8) and isogeometric elements (Bézier surfaces and triangles) are supported. In the latter case, high-order meshes of any degree can be generated.
